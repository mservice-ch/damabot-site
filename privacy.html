Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This policy explains how the damabot app (“the app”, “we”) handles your information. damabot is operated by M Services GmbH. We designed the app to collect as little as possible: there is no advertising, no third-party analytics, and we never sell your data.

Information we collect

  • Account. You sign in with Sign in with Apple. We receive an account identifier and an email address used to create your account. Your email is never shown to other users.
  • Display name (optional). If you add a name, it is shown only on channels you choose to publish to Explore.
  • Your channels and preferences. The subjects, sources, voice, language, length and house-style rules you configure, so we can generate your episodes.
  • Generated content. The audio episodes and transcripts created for you, and the cover images you upload for a channel.
  • Push token. If you enable notifications, a device push token so we can tell you when an episode is ready.

The app does not track you across other apps or websites and contains no advertising or analytics SDKs. We do not access your contacts, photos (beyond an image you explicitly pick for a channel), location, or microphone.

How we use it

  • To authenticate you and keep your account.
  • To research, write and narrate the podcast episodes you request.
  • To deliver episodes and, if enabled, send “episode ready” notifications.
  • To show channels you publish — and channels others publish — in Explore.

Publishing to Explore

Publishing is off by default. If you turn on “Publish to Explore” for a channel, that channel’s name, description, subjects, cover image, your display name, and its ready episodes become visible to other damabot users. Your email address is never published. You can make a channel private again at any time.

Where your data lives

Account data, preferences and generated audio are stored with our backend provider Supabase. To produce episodes we send your channel’s configured subjects, sources and rules to text-generation and text-to-speech services (Anthropic for writing and Microsoft Azure / Edge for voice synthesis). Sign in with Apple and push notifications are provided by Apple. These providers process data on our behalf to deliver the service.

To save space, older episodes’ audio may be automatically removed from the cloud (you choose how many to keep); the episode and its transcript remain, and anything you downloaded stays on your device.

Your choices and rights

  • Edit or delete your channels, episodes and downloads in the app at any time.
  • Turn notifications on or off in iOS Settings.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (below).

Children

damabot is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new “last updated” date.

Contact

Questions or data requests: contact@m-services.ch.
